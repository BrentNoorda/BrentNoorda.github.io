TicketShare Bay
Where live music fans share their concert tickets.

Tickets are meant to be shared. Music is for the fans, not the music-hating, money-grubbing concert promoters. A concert is a public expression--free speech, if you will--and free speech must remain protected AND FREE.

How we operate: It's simple. One person buys a concert ticket and sends that one ticket TicketShare Bay. We quickly analyze the paper and ink composition, digitize that information, send the original ticket back to the original fan, create flawless reproductions of that ticket (with different seat or serial numbers), then send those reproductions to anyone who wants one.

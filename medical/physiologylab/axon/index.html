AXON, this membrane action potential simulation of the Hodgin-Huxley model for excitable tissue, was written for DOS by Dr. Robert I. Macey, Nathan Sato, David Hanzel, and Michael Liu. It runs on the js-dos emulator from Guryanov Aleksander. A keyboard is needed for this emulator, and so a purely touch-screen device probably won't do.

Simulation of the Hodgkin-Huxley model for excitable tissue.

Axon, this membrane action potential simulation of the Hodgkin-Huxley model for excitable tissue, was written for DOS by Dr. Robert I. Macey, Nathan Sato, David Hanzel, and Michael Liu. It runs on the js-dos emulator from Guryanov Aleksander.

A keyboard is needed for this emulator, and so a purely touch-screen device probably won't do. Note: The minus key ('-') may not work on all browsers. Try Firefox.

The short URL for this page is catchy.link/AxonActionPotential. For questions contact Brent Noorda.
